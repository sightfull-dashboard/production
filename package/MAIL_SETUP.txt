Sightfull SMTP setup

Add these values to your .env file:

SMTP_HOST=smtp.sightfull.co.za
SMTP_PORT=465
SMTP_SECURE=true
SMTP_USER=dashboard@sightfull.co.za
SMTP_PASS=YOUR_MAILBOX_PASSWORD
SMTP_FROM_NAME=Sightfull Dashboard
SMTP_FROM_EMAIL=dashboard@sightfull.co.za

Notes:
- Port 465 uses secure=true.
- Payroll submission emails will send to the client's payroll email and CC the client's payroll CC when configured.
- The app will still save the payroll submission even if the email send fails.
